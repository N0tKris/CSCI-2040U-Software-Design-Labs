# PlateRate – User Guide

This guide covers everything a regular user needs to browse restaurants and share reviews on PlateRate.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open PlateRate in your browser (default: `http://127.0.0.1:5000`).
2. On the landing page, click **User View**.
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. Enter a username and password, then submit.
5. You will be redirected to your dashboard after registration.

### Logging In

1. From the landing page, click **User View**.
2. Click **Sign In**.
3. Enter your credentials and click **Sign In**.
4. You will be redirected to the User Dashboard.

---

## Browsing Restaurants

After logging in, the User Dashboard displays all available restaurants in a card-style grid. Each card shows:

- Restaurant name
- Cuisine type
- Location
- Star rating (if imported from Yelp)
- Dietary tags (if available)

You can scroll through the catalog to explore all listings.

---

## Searching & Filtering

Use the **search bar** at the top of the dashboard to find restaurants by name, cuisine type, or location. Type your query and click **Search** to filter results.

---

## Viewing Restaurant Details

1. Click **View Details** on any restaurant card.
2. A detailed panel opens showing the restaurant's name, location, description, and all published reviews.
3. If the restaurant was imported from Yelp, a Yelp rating and review snippets may also appear.

---

## Leaving a Review

You must be logged in to leave a review.

1. On the restaurant card or detail panel, click **Leave Review**.
2. A modal window appears.
3. Select your **star rating** (1–5 stars).
4. Type your **review comment** in the text field.
5. Click **Submit Review**.

Your review is saved to the database and displayed on the restaurant page immediately.

---

## Logging Out

Click **Sign out** in the top-right corner of any page to end your session.

---

## Notes

- You do not need to be logged in to browse restaurants or view published reviews.
- Each user can submit multiple reviews for different restaurants.
- Reviews are published immediately upon submission (no admin approval required in the current MVP).
