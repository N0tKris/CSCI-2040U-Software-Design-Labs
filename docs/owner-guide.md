# PlateRate – Owner Guide

This guide covers everything a restaurant owner needs to set up and manage their listing on PlateRate.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Owner Account

1. Open PlateRate in your browser (default: `http://127.0.0.1:5000`).
2. On the landing page, click **Owner View**.
3. Click **Owner Sign Up**.
4. Enter a username and password, then submit.
5. You will be logged in and redirected to the Owner Dashboard.

### Logging In

1. From the landing page, click **Owner View**.
2. Click **Owner Sign In**.
3. Enter your credentials and click **Sign In**.
4. You will be redirected to the Owner Dashboard.

---

## Creating Your Restaurant Listing

Each owner account can register **one restaurant**. To create your listing:

1. From the Owner Dashboard, locate the **Register Your Restaurant** form.
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Restaurant Name** — the public-facing name of your restaurant
   - **Cuisine** — e.g., Italian, Japanese, Canadian
   - **Location** — street address or general area
   - **Dietary Tags** *(optional)* — e.g., vegan, gluten-free
   - **Description** *(optional)* — a short description for customers
3. Click **Register Restaurant**.

Your restaurant will appear in your dashboard and in the public restaurant catalog immediately.

---

## Uploading a Restaurant Image

A photo helps customers recognize your listing.

1. From the Owner Dashboard, find your restaurant listing.
2. Click **Upload Image**.
3. Select an image file from your computer.
   - Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
   - Maximum file size: 5 MB
4. Submit the form.

The image will appear on your restaurant card and detail page.

---

## Adding Menu Items

1. From the Owner Dashboard, locate your restaurant.
2. Click **Add Menu Item** (or equivalent button).
3. Fill in the item name, price, and description.
4. Submit the form.

Menu items will be visible to users viewing your restaurant's detail page.

---

## Viewing Your Reviews

Customer reviews for your restaurant are visible in your Owner Dashboard. Reviews are published immediately when customers submit them.

---

## Logging Out

Click **Sign out** in the top-right corner of any page to end your session.

---

## Important Limitations

- Each owner account is limited to **one restaurant**. If you need to manage multiple locations, create separate owner accounts.
- Only admins can edit or delete your restaurant listing after it is created. Contact your platform administrator if changes are needed.
